Hello! Welcome to my webpage


I'm a grad student at IISER Pune, India working for my Ph.D. in Experimental High energy Physics. Our group is a part of the CMS collaboration based on CMS experiment, a multipurpose detector at the Large Hadron Collider, CERN. My work focuses on exploring beyond the standard model (BSM) physics signatures using the recorded high energetic proton-proton collisions and dedicated CMS software improvements.
Apart from the academic activities, I like to explore coding world, an avid football fan and a keen traveler who has a great interest in photography, music, politics, and diverse culture.

This webpage is mostly non-academic.
